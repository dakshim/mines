The classic Minesweeper has just found a new home in the open web! Though the app has been developed keeping mobile platform in mind, you can play it in any modern browser (like Firefox!), mobile or desktop.

Objective

Objective of this game is to clear the board/cells without detonating the mines hidden. Cells have indicating number or clues which tells the number of Mines in contact with the cells. Color Codes
  • Blue Cell is the default cell.
  • Gray Cell is an empty cell.
  • Red Cell is detonated mine cell
  • Orange/Yellow Cell is the cell flagged by you

Controls

  • Tap in any cell to reveal itself
  • To set a flag, tap & hold for a while. Or right-click if you have mouse
